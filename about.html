About Akron Crossdock Warehouse

We provide same-day cross-dock, transloading, pallet rework/recoup, and short-term storage (store/hold) for LTL and truckload freight. Our team focuses on fast turns, clear communication, and photo/BOL documentation so carriers, brokers, and shippers can keep schedules on track.

Services at a glance

  • Cross-Dock & Transfer (live unload/load, trailer-to-trailer/van/box)
  • Transloading between vehicles or modes
  • Rework & Recoup (re-stack, re-wrap, banding, corner boards, relabel)
  • Store / Hold (daily/weekly short-term storage)
  • BOL prep, photo proof, counts

FAQs

Do you accept LTL and full truckload?

Yes — we handle both LTL and TL (FAK).

Can you rework shifted or leaning pallets?

Yes — re-stack, re-wrap, banding, and corner boards with photo proof.

Do you offer short-term storage?

Yes — daily or weekly store/hold with handling and cycle counts as needed.

How do I schedule after-hours?

Call ahead to arrange an appointment outside 8–5.
