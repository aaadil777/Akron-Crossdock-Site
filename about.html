About Akron Crossdock Warehouse

We operate a 19,000 ft² warehouse in Akron providing same-day cross-dock, pallet rework/recoup, and short- and long-term storage. We are open for all types of freight and focus on fast turns, clear communication, and photo/BOL documentation to keep brokers, carriers, and shippers on schedule.

Services at a glance

  • Cross-Dock & Transfer (trailer↔trailer/van/box)
  • Transloading between vehicles or modes
  • Rework & Recoup (re-stack, re-wrap, banding, corner boards, relabel)
  • Storage: daily/weekly/monthly (short- & long-term)
  • BOL prep, photo proof, counts

Coverage

Same-day: Akron, Cleveland, Canton, Youngstown. Statewide: Columbus, Toledo, Cincinnati.

Driver notes

  • Check in on arrival for dock door & safety notes.
  • Have BOL and seal ready; we record photos and counts.
  • After-hours by appointment.
