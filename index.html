Cross-dock • Transload • Rework • Storage
Akron, Ohio

19,000 ft² facility delivering same-day cross-dock and pallet rework with short- and long-term storage. We’re open for all types of freight and keep loads moving on schedule.

Services

Cross-Dock & Transfer

Live unload/load — trailer-to-trailer or trailer-to-box-truck. Fast turns to keep schedules on track.

  • Late cutoff / appointment rescue
  • Seal control, photos & BOL
  • LTL/TL, FAK handling

Transloading

Move freight between vehicles or modes (53’, box truck, van) to hit the next leg on time.

  • Pallet swap & redistribution
  • Door-to-door staging
  • Final-mile handoff

Rework & Recoup

Fix shifted or damaged pallets to protect product and prevent claims.

  • Re-stacking, re-wrapping, bands & corner boards
  • Labeling, pallet builds
  • Counts, photos, incident notes

Storage (Short & Long-Term)

Staging between appointments or extended hold — pay only for what you use in our 19,000 ft² warehouse.

  • Daily/weekly/monthly pallet storage
  • Inbound checks & cycle counts
  • Scheduled outbound prep
Open for all freight: retail, food & beverage, consumer goods, metals, packaging, and more.
